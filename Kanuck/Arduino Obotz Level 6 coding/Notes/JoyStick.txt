Joystick:

Converts physical movements to electrical signals to the micro controller 
it converts movments in two axis: x - axis (L to R), y - axis (U to D)

ADC - Analog to Digital conversion 

Range: 0 - 1023

Joystick movements:

x axis :

Left: 1023
Center: ~ 512
Right: 0

y axis :

Up: 1023
Center: ~ 512
Down: 0

A2 (Y axis- up and down) and A3 (X axis - Left and Right) PINS of your joystick are analog PINS
